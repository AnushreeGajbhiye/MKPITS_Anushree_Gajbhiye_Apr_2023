About Us

Our Specialities

Super Speciality Hospital (SSH) Nagpur, is a super speciality hospital which encompasses and provides comprehensive health care. SSH offers healthcare services in Cardiology, Cardiovascular & Thoracic surgeries, Nephrology, Urology, Neurology, Neurosurgery, Gastroenterology, Radio diagnosis, Anaesthesiology, Pathology, Blood Bank, Biochemistry and Microbiology. SSH Is well equipped with advanced Laboratories, state-of-the- art Operation theaters(OT) and Intensive Care Units(ICU) The hospital has full-fledged In-Patient (IPD) and Out-Patient (OPD) departments. SSH is all about caring for patients and lives by its core vision of providing excellent healthcare services by leveraging on technology and innovative procedures.

Vision

To bring healthcare of International standards to this region, to evolve as a premier hospital in the country and to TOUCH the lives of the people we serve through excellence in clinical care, quality and commitment.

Mission

To provide Hope, Care and Cure. To provide compassionate, accessible, high quality, cost effective healthcare to one all. To serve with a patient's first motto and to work towards a patient- centered care.
